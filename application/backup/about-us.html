About Us

What We Do

We believed that people will feel good when they look good and this should applies to everyone.

Thus we have a group of experienced hairstylist to start a salon that not only offers professional hair services for our customers, but also to have subsidized services for the disadvantaged, vulnerable or special needs, which are partly funded with our pay-it-forward programme.

Currently, we offer a full range of trending hair services for all hairdressing needs: cut, style, dye, treatment etc. We are partnered with established brands like Shiseido, L'oreal, Uppecut to offer a range of products for our customers to choose from.

We too have started a mobile haircut service for those who face inconveniences moving around. Prices are reasonable as we are aware that what the general public consider as a basic service may not be so for those who face logistics challenges.


Motto

Kindness with dignity is our motto. Our hope is that people pay kindness forward in every little way to brighten up the society as a whole. This can start as an opportunity to sponsor a haircut to the needy/disadvantaged and/or financially distressed.
